PIN

Before you start, you need to create (and copy) a PIN which you'll use in a few moments of time. The PIN should consist of the following elements:
  1. The two first letters of your mother's first name,
  2. The first digit of your current house number and
  3. the first three digits of your mobile phone number.
Example: My mother is called Doris, I currently live in Torstrasse 25 and my mobile number is 078 302 78 67. Hence, my individual PIN is: DO2078
